Leaf SMP is a community-focused vanilla survival server.  
By joining, you agree to follow the rules below.

---

## 1. Respect All Players

- No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks  
- No hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language  
- No sexual or explicit content  

General swearing is permitted.  
Excessive or targeted abuse toward other players is not allowed.

---

## 2. No Cheating or Exploiting

- No hacked clients or unauthorized modifications  
- No duplication glitches or bug abuse  
- No X-ray, fly hacks, or unfair gameplay advantages  

Leaf SMP is strictly fair-play survival.

---

## 3. No Griefing or Stealing

- Do not destroy or modify builds without permission  
- Do not steal from chests or player bases  
- Respect claimed or clearly occupied areas  

---

## 4. No Real-Money Gambling

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Casinos or gambling mechanics  
- Coin flip or betting systems  
- Any form of real or premium currency wagering  

---

## Enforcement

Server staff may enforce rules at their discretion to maintain a safe and fair environment.

Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.
