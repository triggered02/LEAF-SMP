Leaf SMP is committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and fair environment for all players.

By joining the server, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Standards

Players are expected to:

- Treat others with respect
- Communicate in a civil and constructive manner
- Play fairly and honestly
- Respect player builds, land, and property
- Follow staff instructions when given

General swearing is permitted. Targeted harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior is not.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Sexual or explicit content
- Cheating, exploiting bugs, or using unauthorized modifications
- Griefing, stealing, or intentional disruption
- Real-money gambling systems or betting mechanics

---

## Enforcement

Server staff are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent bans

Staff decisions are made to maintain fairness and community safety.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness misconduct, contact server management through the official support email or website.
